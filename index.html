About



We work as one unit and raise donations for Developing countries in the world

Our approach is rooted in years of on-the-ground experience, driven by a careful methodology and powered by a deep passion to improve the lives of children and give them a chance to grow up in a family.

We believe in collaboration as we know this work is bigger than any one organization. We are part of a global network of nonprofit organizations leading the worldwide movement.

Our mission and follow ups

Our mission is to help the poor children survive in miserable and helpless situation by raising funds through donation camp . The procedure could be either digitally or in offline mode through camps and various events organised locally. We have been authenticated by Sparks Foundation and verified through it's terms and conditions.

Doing this service does not force anyone of us and is same to all to help .It is completely upto others to give us their hands on it. We appreciate all efforts and good intentions who wants to make this mission to be successful.
